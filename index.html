About Me

Hi! I'm Maria Cruz

With over five years of professional experience, I specialize in crafting modern, minimalist, and sustainable interior designs. My approach blends functionality with aesthetics, turning ordinary spaces into warm, elegant environments. I believe that thoughtful design should not only look good but also serve a purpose in people’s everyday lives. Throughout my career, I’ve worked with homeowners, entrepreneurs, and commercial clients to bring their visions to life. I take pride in creating interiors that reflect personality while maximizing comfort and usability. I enjoy collaborating closely with clients to ensure that each space tells a story. Whether it's a cozy living room or a professional office, I tailor each design to suit individual lifestyles and needs. I’m inspired by the way design can transform the way people feel and live within their spaces. My goal is to deliver interiors that are timeless, efficient, and beautifully livable. Let’s work together to create a space that truly feels like home.
